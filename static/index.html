SkyTracer Features & Pipeline

Multi-Dimensional Dest Explore: Scans dozens of destination routes in parallel over variable trip durations (e.g. 5, 7, or 10 days) using mid-date averages.

Calendar Price Matrix: Generates pricing heatmaps comparing departure vs return combinations to spot pricing anomalies.
